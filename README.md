# 📊 Super Store Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Super Store sales performance. It helps business users monitor sales, profit, quantity, shipping modes, customer segments, payment methods, and monthly trends.


## 🚀 Features

- Region-wise filtering (Central, East, South, West)
- KPI Cards
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Total Quantity
- Monthly Sales Trend (YoY)
- Monthly Profit Trend (YoY)
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Sub-Category
- Sales by Ship Mode
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Sales by Payment Mode
- Interactive slicers and cross-filtering

## 🎯 Business Insights

- Office Supplies generated the highest sales.
- Standard Class shipping contributes the largest revenue.
- Consumer segment has the highest sales.
- COD is the most preferred payment mode.
- Phones are the highest-selling sub-category.
- Sales and profit peak during the last quarter of the year.
