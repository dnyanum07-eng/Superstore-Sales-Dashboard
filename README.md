# Superstore-Sales-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard project using Superstore-Sales Dataset 

An interactive and data-driven Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and track the sales performance, profit margins, and customer behavior of a retail Super Store. This dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends across different regions, product categories, shipping modes, and time periods.
Dashboard Overview

 Key Features & Performance Metrics
The dashboard tracks core business KPIs and breaks down operational performance through various granular visualizations:
1. High-Level KPIs
Sum of Sales:$2M
 * **Sum of Profit:** $175K
 * **Sum of Quantity Sold:** 22K units
 * 
### 2. Strategic Breakdowns
 * **Sales by Segment:** Analyzes customer types, highlighting **Consumer** as the largest driver (48%), followed by Corporate (33%) and Home Office (19%).
 * **Sales by Payment Mode:** Tracks transactional preferences, showing **Cash on Delivery (COD)** in the lead at 43%, followed by Online (35%) and Cards (22%).
 * **Product Categories & Sub-Categories:**
   * **Top Categories:** Office Supplies ($0.64M) leads sales, followed closely by Technology ($0.47M) and Furniture ($0.45M).
   * **Top Sub-Categories:** Highlights specific top performers like Phones ($0.20M), Chairs ($0.18M), and Binders ($0.17M).
 * **Sales by Ship Mode:** Tracks logistics preference, showing a massive reliance on **Standard Class** ($0.91M) compared to Second Class, First Class, and Same Day options.
 * 
### 3. Year-Over-Year (YoY) Time-Series Analysis
 * **Monthly Profit by YoY:** A dual-line chart mapping profitability fluctuations month-by-month, showcasing critical peaks in March, September, October, and December.
 * **Month Sales by YoY:** Visualizes monthly sales volume trajectories to help identify seasonal demand spikes and annual trends.
## 🛠️ Built With

 * **Power BI Desktop** – For data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard visualization.
 * **Power Query** – For data cleaning, transformation, and ETL processes.
 * **Theme:** Customized Dark Mode UI for sleek, high-contrast executive reporting.
 * 
 Key Business Insights Derived
 1. **Seasonality Trajectory:** Both sales and profits see significant spikes during the final quarter of the year (Q4), specifically around October and December, likely due to holiday shopping seasons.
 2. **Payment Preferences:** Cash on Delivery remains the dominant payment choice (43%). Introducing incentives for card or online payments could optimize cash flow operations.
 3. **Logistical Dominance:** Overwhelming preference for Standard Class shipping suggests customers prioritize cost-efficiency over delivery speed.
